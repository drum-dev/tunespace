About Us

TuneSpace is a group project from 5 students at the University of Washington coding bootcamp. They are Mark Drummond, Morgan Sherrill, Jefrey Colegrove, Kanwara Moore, and Dan Golden. Our goal of this project was to create a musical guide that provides biographies, discographies, and lyrics of musicians in all genres. By pulling data from various data bases we created a centralized hub where users can explore their favorite artists and discover new music.
